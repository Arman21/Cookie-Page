Bavarian Motor Works,commonly referred to as BMW,is a German multinational company which produces luxury vehicles and motorcycles.The company was founded in 1916 as a manufacturer of aircraft engines,which it produced from 1917 until 1918 and again from 1933 to 1945.Automobiles are marketed under the brands BMW, Mini and Rolls-Royce, and motorcycles are marketed under the brand BMW Motorrad.In 2015,BMW was the world's twelfth-largest producer of motor vehicles, with 2,279,503 vehicles produced.
